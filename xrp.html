Ripple(XRP)

XRP is a digital asset native to the XRP Ledger (XRPL), an open-source, decentralized platform designed specifically for fast, cost-effective, and efficient global financial transactions. Its primary function is to serve as a bridge currency to facilitate cross-border payments, offering a faster and cheaper alternative to traditional systems
